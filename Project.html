Miniature w/ Light Wiring

This is the wooden miniature house we built, complete with gates, artificial grass, and a little fence. The house lights up cleanly and has a more realistic appearance due to the LED lights we installed inside and the wiring hidden through the ceiling.

Line Follower Car

This is the line follower car we built, complete with a unique frame, roof rack, and window holes. To enable the car to move, we installed arduino, motors and wiring inside, and we constructed a wooden platform for the wheels. Although it is little, handcrafted model, it is completely functional and made to resemble an actual off-road vehicle.
